Adulting-level bill system (variable income / moving)
Use these three buckets so you never get cash-flow trapped:
  • Must-Pay: rent, insurance, minimum debt payments
  • Must-Run: utilities, phone, internet, groceries
  • Nice-to-Have: subscriptions, extras, upgrades
Rule: fund Must-Pay + Must-Run first every payday, then discretionary spending last.
Optional: store your bill inventory as a JSON object for your dashboard later:

  "bills": [
